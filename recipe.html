Here is a simple and classic recipe for making pasta from scratch:


Ingredients:


  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed)

    • Instructions:


    1. Prepare the Dough:


    • On a clean work surface, make a mound with the flour and create a well in the center.
    • Crack the eggs into the well and add salt.
    • Using a fork, beat the eggs and slowly incorporate the flour from the edges of the well.
    • Continue mixing until the dough begins to come together.
    • Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough is too dry, add a few drops of water; if too sticky, add a little more flour.
